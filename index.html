The reality

Running an independent restaurant is as complex as running a chain.
You have never had the support to match.

Chains have always had an advantage that has nothing to do with the quality of their food or the talent of their operators. They have a head office. An ops team. Specialists on energy, staffing, compliance, cost control. People whose job it is to watch the numbers, catch the problems, and tell them what to do next.

Independent operators have always had to be all of those people at once. This is not a problem of operators making bad decisions. It is a problem of operators making hard decisions without adequate support — in conditions that have become almost impossibly difficult.

What we built

The Plate gives independent restaurants the intelligence, support and guidance that only large groups have ever had.

Not a tool that sits alongside your business. The head office you never had. A business coach that knows your numbers. Energy buying power at rates chains get, with someone watching every bill. Staffing that finds the right people and makes them affordable. Everything sequenced so that from day one, you have something genuinely useful — and everything that follows builds on it.

01
Today

The ops team in your pocket.

A business coach that knows your industry, available on WhatsApp, from day one. Send a photo of your menu and find out exactly where your margin is going. Upload a supplier invoice and find out if you are being overcharged. Ask anything — licensing, pricing, staffing, compliance. Get back analysis and recommendations that would cost hundreds from a consultant, in plain English, in minutes. No new platform to learn. No integrations. No onboarding. Just WhatsApp.

02
Next

A service that gets to know your business.

The more you use it, the more it works for you. Until it starts telling you things you did not know to ask — flagging compliance deadlines, cost anomalies, and opportunities specific to your venue before they become problems.

03
Later

The complete head office.

Energy buying power through partnerships that buy at rates the open market cannot match, with forensic monitoring of every bill. Culture-matched staffing that finds people who will still be with you next winter, with wage subsidy support that most operators have never even heard of. And an orchestration layer that synthesises it all and tells you exactly what to do next.
